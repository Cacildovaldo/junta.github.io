About Fred Token

Built for Sustainable Growth on Polygon

Fred Token (FRED) is a next-generation utility token built on the Polygon MATIC network, designed with long-term sustainability and community growth in mind. With a total supply of 1 billion tokens, Fred Token implements innovative liquidity solutions to ensure price stability and reduce volatility.

Our presale is structured to accumulate sufficient liquidity from the start, with 60% of tokens allocated to the presale and 20% dedicated to locked liquidity. This approach creates a strong foundation for the token's ecosystem.
